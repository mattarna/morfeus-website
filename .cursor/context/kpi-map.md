# KPI Map

## Primary (90-day focus)
1. **Call booked** — the #1 metric. Everything on the site is built to drive here.
2. **ROIometro completed** — pre-conversion mechanism. How many CEOs complete the calculation.
3. **CTR homepage → service pages** — measures if routing/smistamento works.

## Secondary
- Lead volume (form submissions)
- Time on page (engagement signal, not a target)
- Bounce rate per page type
- Sales (depends on commercial team, not on the site)

## Per-Page KPI Assignment
| Page | Primary KPI | Secondary KPI |
|------|------------|---------------|
| Homepage | CTR to service pages, ROIometro completions | Time on page, scroll depth |
| Service pages | Call booked (CTA click → Calendly) | Time on page, scroll to CTA |
| Case study pages | Call booked (CTA click → Calendly) | Read completion rate |
| Funnel pages | Conversion per step (defined in funnel config) | Drop-off rate per step |

## Conversion Flow
Homepage (impression) → Service page (education) → Call booked (conversion)
Homepage (impression) → ROIometro (engagement) → Call booked (conversion)
Funnel (paid traffic) → Multi-step → Call booked / purchase
