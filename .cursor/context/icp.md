# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Primary Target
CEO / Founder / C-level of mid-market companies (10-200 employees) who:
- Know AI is important but don't know where to start operationally
- Have tried tools or consultants that delivered slides, not results
- Want measurable impact, not theoretical frameworks
- Are willing to invest in structural change, not quick fixes

## Awareness Level
Problem-aware to solution-aware. They know something is broken (inefficiency, manual processes, lost value) but haven't found a partner they trust to fix it systematically.

## Pain Points
- "We bought AI tools but nobody uses them"
- "Our processes are manual and we're losing money but we don't know where"
- "Consultants gave us a deck, then disappeared"
- "We need someone who stays and delivers, not sells and leaves"

## Decision Drivers
- Proof (numbers, case studies, measurable results)
- Low-risk entry (pilot, 30-day exit clause)
- Credibility (who else has done this?)
- Speed to first result
