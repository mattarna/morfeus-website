# Tone of Voice

## Character
Direct, confident, technical but accessible. We speak like a senior partner in a boardroom — not like a marketing agency on social media.

## Rules
- Use active voice, short sentences
- Be specific: numbers > adjectives, proof > promises
- Never use buzzwords without substance ("leverage", "synergy", "game-changer")
- Technical language is OK when talking to executives — they respect precision
- Italian copy is the primary language; English is a professional translation, not a creative rewrite

## What We Sound Like
- "We found 340 hours/month of manual work. We automated 280 of them in 6 weeks."
- "Your organization leaks value in places you can't see. We measure it."

## What We Do NOT Sound Like
- "Revolutionize your business with cutting-edge AI solutions!"
- "Unlock the power of artificial intelligence for your team!"
- "We are passionate about helping companies transform digitally"

## Copy Hierarchy
1. Headline: bold, uppercase, punchy — max 8-10 words
2. Subheadline: context, stakes, curiosity — max 2 sentences
3. Body: structured, scannable, evidence-based
4. CTA: action-oriented, specific ("Prenota la tua analisi" not "Scopri di piu")
